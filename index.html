üìö Quick Answer Key

High-Yield Safety Priorities:
  • Neuro: Airway, LOC, seizure precautions
  • Psych: Suicide risk, serotonin syndrome, NMS
  • Pain: Respiratory depression, oversedation
  • Inflammation: GI bleed, renal injury
  • Endocrine: Hypoglycemia, DKA/HHS
  • Infection: Anaphylaxis, nephrotoxicity

üí° Facilitation Tips: Keep groups focused on SAFETY FIRST thinking. Push them to justify WHY their priority is #1. Challenge them with "What kills the patient fastest?" Encourage cross-system connections (e.g., opioid + benzo = respiratory arrest). Use the timer to maintain energy and urgency!
